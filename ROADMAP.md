## Core Principles — Never Negotiable

These will never change regardless of funding, scale, or pressure:

- ✅ No content is ever auto-deleted — context labels only
- ✅ All verdicts stored immutably on IPFS — publicly auditable
- ✅ Algorithm weights public and community-governed
- ✅ Zero PII for anonymous and whistleblower accounts
- ✅ Full platform open source — MIT License — forever
- ✅ No advertising model — ever
