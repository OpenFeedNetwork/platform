HOW IT WORKS

Built different.
By design.

01 · ANTI-SUPPRESSION

Content cannot be hidden

suppress_post: false is hardcoded into the platform architecture. No administrator, no algorithm, and no governance vote can change this. Your content reaches your audience. Every time.

02 · BLOCKCHAIN AUDIT

Every decision is public

Every moderation decision, every Candor Shield verdict, every governance vote is stored on IPFS and recorded on the Polygon blockchain. Anyone can verify any decision. Forever.

03 · COMMUNITY GOVERNANCE

You govern the algorithm

Token holders vote on algorithm parameters. The community sets the rules the algorithm follows — not executives optimizing for ad revenue. Every vote is on-chain and permanent.

04 · ZERO KNOWLEDGE

Privacy by architecture

Age verification uses zero-knowledge proofs — we confirm you meet requirements without learning who you are. No PII collected. No data to sell. No surveillance.

05 · DECENTRALIZED STORAGE

Content stored forever

All content is stored on IPFS and Arweave — decentralized networks that no single entity controls. Your posts cannot be deleted by a server shutdown or a corporate decision.

06 · OPEN SOURCE

Every line of code is public

The entire platform — every microservice, every API, every smart contract — is open source on GitHub. Anyone can audit, fork, and verify that we do exactly what we say we do.
