Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 19, 2026  ·  Open Feed Network, Inc.  ·  legal@openfeed.network

Plain English summary: We collect the minimum data possible. We never sell your data. We never run ads. Age verification uses zero-knowledge proofs — we confirm you're eligible without ever learning who you are.

1. Data We Collect

Account data

  • Standard accounts: Username, email address, hashed password
  • Anonymous accounts: Username and hashed password only — no email required
  • Whistleblower accounts: End-to-end encrypted — no PII collected or stored under any circumstances

Content data

  • Posts, comments, and media you create are stored on IPFS and Arweave
  • Content stored on decentralized networks is permanent — it cannot be deleted by Open Feed Network or by you after publication
  • Moderation decisions affecting your content are stored on the Polygon blockchain — permanently public and auditable

Usage data

  • IP address — anonymized after 24 hours
  • Device type and browser — aggregated only, never tied to individual identity
  • Interaction patterns — used solely for feed algorithm transparency reporting

2. Data We Never Collect

We never
  • Collect payment card data (handled entirely by third-party processors)
  • Collect PII for age verification (zero-knowledge proofs only)
  • Track your browsing activity outside the Platform
  • Build advertising profiles or sell data to advertisers
  • Sell your personal data to any third party for any reason
  • Use your data to train AI models without explicit opt-in consent

3. Children's Privacy (COPPA / KOSA / GDPR-K)

Guardian Shield provides 7-layer child protection. We comply with:

  • COPPA — Users under 13 require verified parental consent. All child data is deleted within 24 hours of age confirmation.
  • KOSA — Kids Online Safety Act compliant. Guardian Shield monitors and protects minors from harmful content.
  • GDPR-K — Children's privacy rights fully implemented for EU users.
  • FDBR — Florida Digital Bill of Rights compliant.

Zero-knowledge age verification proves eligibility without collecting any personally identifiable information. We never learn who you are — only that you meet the age requirement.

4. Data Sharing

We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • NCMEC CyberTipline — CSAM is immediately reported as required by 18 U.S.C. § 2258A. This is a legal obligation.
  • FBI IC3 — Terrorism content confirmed by GIFCT hash matching is reported automatically within 24 hours.
  • Legal process — We comply with valid court orders and subpoenas. We will notify you of legal process unless prohibited by law.
  • Infrastructure providers — Cloud and security vendors under strict data processing agreements. They process data on our behalf only.

We do not share data with advertisers, data brokers, or analytics companies.

5. Blockchain & IPFS

Important: Moderation decisions, governance votes, and content safety verdicts stored on IPFS and the Polygon blockchain are public and permanent by design. This data cannot be deleted. Do not include personal information in content you intend to be permanently public.

This public audit trail is a feature, not a bug — it is what makes Open Feed Network the most transparent platform in existence. Every moderation action can be independently verified by anyone, forever.

6. Your Rights

  • Access — Request a copy of your personal data at any time
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your account data (note: IPFS/blockchain content is permanent by design)
  • Correction — Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Portability — Export your data in machine-readable format
  • Objection — Object to processing of your personal data
  • Restriction — Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances

To exercise any right, email legal@openfeed.network. We respond within 30 days.

7. Security

  • All data in transit encrypted with TLS 1.3
  • All data at rest encrypted with AES-256
  • Whistleblower accounts use end-to-end encryption — we cannot read your content
  • Security policy published at github.com/OpenFeedNetwork/platform/SECURITY.md
  • Regular security testing and vulnerability assessment

In the event of a data breach affecting your personal data, we will notify you within 72 hours of discovery.

8. International Data Transfers

Open Feed Network is operated from Florida, United States. If you access the Platform from the EU, UK, or other regions, your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States. We rely on EU Standard Contractual Clauses and the UK International Data Transfer Agreement for such transfers. A copy is available at openfeed.network/legal/sccs.

9. California Residents (CCPA / CPRA)

California residents have additional rights under CCPA/CPRA:

  • Right to know what personal information we collect and how it is used
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt out of sale of personal information — we do not sell personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising CCPA rights
  • Right to correct inaccurate personal information
  • Right to limit use of sensitive personal information

To exercise California rights, email legal@openfeed.network with subject line "CCPA Request."

10. Contact & Data Protection Officer

For all privacy questions, data requests, or concerns:

  • Email: legal@openfeed.network
  • Data Protection Officer: legal@openfeed.network
  • Response time: Within 30 days for all requests
  • Mailing: Open Feed Network, Inc. · Florida, United States

We take every privacy request seriously. United We Stand. 💙
