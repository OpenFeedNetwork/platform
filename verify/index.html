CANDOR VERIFY · REAL-TIME BROADCAST INTEGRITY

Truth-rating live feeds, podcasts, and broadcasts as they happen

Candor Verify ingests live audio and video streams, transcribes speech in real time, extracts factual claims, and delivers a CANDOR score verdict within 3–4 seconds of the spoken word — stored permanently on IPFS and the Polygon blockchain.

A paid influencer with direct access to the live feed can game the algorithm from the inside — using money, not community votes, to shape what people see and believe. Audiences cannot tell the difference. Platforms cannot detect it in real time. Candor Verify closes that gap — permanently.

The Real-Time Pipeline

Six sequential stages — end-to-end latency of 3–4 seconds from spoken word to listener alert

01
Live Stream Ingestion
Audio and video streams are ingested via RTMP, HLS, WebRTC, or direct podcast feed. Supports YouTube Live, Spotify, Apple Podcasts, broadcast news APIs, and custom RTMP endpoints.
Latency: <100ms
02
Real-Time Transcription
Speech-to-text converts the audio stream into a rolling transcript with speaker diarization — identifying who said what, with timestamps accurate to 200ms.
Latency: ~500ms
03
Claim Extraction
NLP identifies factual claims within the transcript stream — distinguishing verifiable assertions from opinion, satire, and speculation. Each claim is tagged with speaker identity and timestamp.
Latency: ~800ms
04
Real-Time Fact-Check
Claims are checked against authoritative databases including government records, peer-reviewed literature, wire services, and the CANDOR network's community-verified knowledge base.
Latency: ~1.5s
05
Influence Pattern Detection
Detects coordinated paid narrative signals — cross-referencing repeated talking points against known paid influence campaigns, lobbying disclosure filings, and financial influence databases.
Latency: ~2s
06
CANDOR Score Verdict + Immutable Storage
A CANDOR score (0–100) is assigned to each verified claim. Verdicts are delivered via webhook to listeners and moderators, then stored permanently on IPFS and recorded on the Polygon blockchain.
Total latency: 3–4 seconds · Tamper-proof forever

The Paid Influence Threat

Why live content requires a new category of protection

🎙️
PODCASTER CAPTURE
Paid sponsors shape talking points that sound like independent opinion. Listeners cannot distinguish sponsored narrative from genuine analysis. Verify detects correlation with known paid campaigns.
📡
LIVE NEWS MANIPULATION
Breaking news segments are especially vulnerable — claims made live cannot be retracted from listener memory. Verify flags unverified claims in real time before they embed as fact.
🗳️
ELECTION INTEGRITY
Coordinated influence operations target live political content during elections. Verify cross-references claims against official election data and flags talking points matching foreign influence patterns.
💊
HEALTH MISINFORMATION
Medical misinformation spread live is the most dangerous category — listeners act on health claims immediately. Verify cross-references all health claims against FDA, CDC, and peer-reviewed databases in real time.
