BEACON FR · FIRST RESPONDER SPECIALIZED

Suicide prevention trained on first responder language

Candor Beacon FR is a specialized detection system trained on the unique linguistic patterns used by law enforcement, firefighters, paramedics, and emergency dispatchers — a population at significantly elevated suicide risk whose distress signals are systematically missed by general-purpose models.

View API Docs Partner With Us
⚠ WHY FIRST RESPONDERS NEED SPECIALIZED DETECTION
First responders are 1.5x more likely to die by suicide than in the line of duty. They use professional language that masks distress, describe crisis differently than civilian populations, and are trained to suppress emotional expression. Standard detection models fail this population at critical moments.

The FR Language Model

Trained on first responder community language patterns

🚒
PROFESSIONAL CODE LANGUAGE
First responders use radio codes, jargon, and professional terminology to discuss crisis states. Beacon FR understands these patterns as potential distress signals.
🧠
MASKED DISTRESS PATTERNS
Trained to detect humor-as-coping, minimization language, bravado masking vulnerability, and indirect expressions of burnout and trauma.
📊
OCCUPATIONAL CONTEXT
Understands shift patterns, critical incident language, PTSD markers specific to first responder trauma exposure, and peer culture dynamics.
