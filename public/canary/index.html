Signal Guide
What changes to this page mean

Different changes to this page signal different things. Learn to read them.

✅
Page updates monthly — all assertions present
The canary is alive. No secret orders have been received. Normal operation. Continue using Candor with confidence.
⚠️
One or more assertions disappear
An assertion was removed, likely because it is no longer true. We cannot say why due to legal constraints. Treat the missing assertion as the signal.
🛑
Page stops updating past the due date
The canary is dead. We have received a secret order that prevents us from maintaining it. This is the strongest signal — treat it seriously.
💀
Page is taken down entirely
The most serious signal. We have been compelled to remove the canary, which itself indicates severe legal pressure. Assume the worst.
