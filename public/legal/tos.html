3. AI-Powered Safety Systems

Candor operates AI-powered safety modules that run continuously. By using Candor, you explicitly consent to your content being processed by these systems. These include:

Truth Shield

Open-source disinformation detection that analyzes post content and assigns transparency scores. Scores are informational — Truth Shield does not automatically remove content.

Sentinel

Automated bot detection and flood protection. Operates silently. Human users will not be affected. Bots and automated scripts will be blocked.

ThreatShield

Real-time content integrity engine analyzing posts for coordinated inauthentic behavior and manipulation. Scores stored internally.

Gateway

Community safety layer that enforces platform-wide content policies silently.

Beacon

Reach analytics system tracking how content spreads. Used to provide honest metrics and detect coordinated amplification.

Shield

Additional protection layer for community members including safeguards for vulnerable users. Methods not publicly disclosed to prevent circumvention.

All safety systems are operated solely to maintain feed integrity. Not used for advertising, data monetization, or political targeting.
